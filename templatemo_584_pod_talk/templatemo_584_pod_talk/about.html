Our story

We invite the world to savor the essence of Filipino drinking culture through a carefully curated selection of our finest alcohol beverages. From the tropical allure of coconut-infused lambanog to the iconic San Miguel Beer, each bottle is a celebration of Filipino pride, crafted to share the warmth, diversity, and joy of our beautiful archipelago with enthusiasts worldwide. Join us in raising a glass to the richness of Filipino traditions, one sip at a time.
